# Volume V: Concurrent Programming

From thread primitives to asynchronous coroutines, from locks to lock-free, from synchronization to tasks—Volume V helps you build a complete understanding of concurrency. Our principle is: **correctness first, then performance; locks first, then lock-free; synchronization first, then tasks**.
